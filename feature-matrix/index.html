Feature Matrix

Warning

This page is under construction, I'll remove it when the page is g2g.

Feature Personal Professional Executive
Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) Fall back to your own OpenAI tokens after your plaform tokens run out.
Primary Email Support Link one Gmail or Outlook account create your account and begin using Clairify.
Multiple Summarization Prompts Different built-in prompts are applied per email class, so summaries fit the type of message.
Custom Summarization Prompts Create or edit prompts and map them to any class for personalized summarization.
Custom Email Classes Define your own classes in addition to the defaults to better reflect your inbox.
Secondary Email Additional Gmail or Outlook email accounts can be connected beyond the primary inbox.
Briefings Digest unread emails of any class so you can catch up on missed messages quickly.
On-Demand Inbox Syncing Fetch email history from a connected inbox.
On-Demand Re-summarization Re-generate summaries immediately after a summarization prompt update.
Topics Group multiple classes by topic and apply view permissions.
Channels A named, dedicated space for messages, just like a label/folder in email, or a Slack/Teams channel. Designed for maximizing awareness while minimizing cognitive load, rather than facilitating communication.
Vendor Channels Receive directly published content from select vendors.
Text-to-Speech Enable audio playback of stories and briefings.
Organizations Add team members to your account to drive organizational alignment.
