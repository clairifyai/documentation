### Gmail Unsubscribe

You can now unsubscribe from mailing lists directly from Clairify. When a sender supports one-click unsubscribe, we'll surface the option so you can get off their list without ever leaving the app.

### Account Visibility

The profile icon in the upper right corner now reflects the active inbox. Previously it always showed the primary account — now it updates as you switch between accounts.

### Upgrade Prompt at Inbox Limit

When you've reached the maximum number of secondary inboxes on your plan, the Add Email Account card now shows an upgrade prompt so you can easily expand.

### Onboarding Actions

Onboarding cards now support all four swipe gestures, matching the behavior of the Instack.

### Subscription Expiry Experience

When your subscription expires, the app now clearly communicates which features are unavailable and prompts you to renew — rather than showing empty or broken UI.

### Bug Fixes

- **Fixed:** The "Sign in successful" toast has been removed — it wasn't providing useful information and made the app feel slower than it is.
- **Fixed:** The three-dot menu no longer appears on the original email view where it doesn't belong.
- **Fixed:** Microsoft login now works correctly, along with a text overflow fix on the login screen.
- **Fixed:** Re-entering an email after an unsupported lookup now correctly shows the action button again.
- **Fixed:** The Microsoft label on the Add Account pop-up now says Outlook instead of Azure.
