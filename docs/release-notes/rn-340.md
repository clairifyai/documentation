### On-Demand Email Audio

Tap any email card to hear it read aloud. If the audio is already cached it plays immediately — otherwise it's generated on the spot. No more reading every card; just listen.

### 24-Hour Email Playlist

Emails received in the last 24 hours are now available as a continuous audio playlist. Think of it as a podcast version of your inbox — listen through without touching the screen. From the web app, choose which categories — Important, Finance, Product, Sales, or your custom groups — are included in your audio playlist. Listen to what matters, skip the rest.

### Automatic Briefing Audio

Morning and evening briefings are now automatically converted to audio so they're ready when you open the app. Consume your briefings hands-free during commutes, between meetings, or while multitasking.

### PDF Attachments (Outlook)

For Outlook inboxes, you can now open PDF attachments directly from Clairify. This was introduced for Gmail in the last release. For emails where the body is just "See attached," you no longer need to leave the app.

### UI/UX Enhancements

- The Briefing channel now supports standard swiping behaviour.
- Briefing actions have been moved to the magic button.
- The Briefing list now shows only unread reports by default.
- Onboarding cards now support free swiping in all directions
- The magic button is no longer activated on onboarding cards since there are no actions available.
- Onboarding cards now display badge numbers for consistent UI alignment.
- Syncing an account now shows an in-progress loading state with informative status messages.
