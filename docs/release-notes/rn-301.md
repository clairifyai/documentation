Clairify has a fresh new logo, wordmark, and color palette. Our iconic octopus is still here — now reimagined as a sleek swirl of tentacles with a more modern, professional feel.

### Faster Load Times

We've made backend optimizations that reduce CPU usage associated with inactive users, which means snappier performance for everyone.

We now record the date and time you accept our privacy policy to support our compliance requirements.

### Subscription Status in Your Profile

Your current plan status is now visible in the Profile section: Trial, Active, Past Due, Cancelled, or Expired.
