### Premium Feature Indicators

Premium features like on-demand sync now display a crown icon on the Account Selection page. When users tap a locked feature, they're guided into the upgrade flow.

### Account Connection Controls

Account connection controls were present in the Settings Panel and on the Home page. Now they are on the home page only. Long press an account to reveal the controls.

### Trial & Subscription Clarity

Users were confused about "30 days free" versus the "7-day premium trial." The trial mechanics and messaging have been updated: All users are on the free tier by default, and can opt-in to a 7-day premium trial. After the premium trial expires, the users are downgraded back to the free tier.

### Renamed "Refresh Account" to "Sync Inbox"

The button formerly labeled "Refresh Account" is now called "Sync Inbox" to make the action clearer.

### GDPR Data Purge

After account deletion, retained email addresses are now automatically purged after 12 months to comply with Microsoft and GDPR data retention requirements.

### Mailroom Filters

In the Mailroom, the Magic Button now highlights the currently active filter.

### Manage Subscription

On the Profile page, the "Manage Subscription" button how has additional states for "Upgrade to Premium" and "Start Free Trial."
